About ICPS 2026

The International Conference of Physics Students (ICPS) 2026 brings together passionate physics students, researchers, and educators from around the world for a week of scientific exchange, cultural immersion, and collaborative exploration. Hosted in the historic and vibrant city of Rabat, Morocco, ICPS 2026 is more than a conference — it’s a celebration of the future of science, learning, and connection.

Venue

ICPS 2026 in Rabat, Morocco’s capital, offers a unique cultural journey where tradition meets modernity. Participants will explore the city’s vibrant medinas, historic landmarks, and coastal charm, while experiencing Moroccan hospitality, cuisine, music, and art. From guided tours of Rabat’s UNESCO World Heritage Sites to evenings filled with local flavors and performances, the week promises a rich immersion into Morocco’s diverse cultural heritage.The conference will be held at Mohammed V University, a prestigious institution located in the heart of Rabat. The venue offers state-of-the-art facilities, beautiful gardens, and a vibrant academic atmosphere, making it the perfect backdrop for this international gathering of physics students.

Travel Info

Essential details to help you prepare for ICPS 2026 in Rabat.

Getting to Rabat

Arrive via Rabat-Salé Airport (RBA), just minutes from downtown, or Casablanca’s Mohammed V Airport (CMN), about an hour by train. Shuttles and taxis available.

Visa Requirements

Many nationalities can enter Morocco visa-free for up to 90 days. Check requirements early and request an invitation letter if needed.

Accommodation

Options from conference hotels, budget hostels, to traditional riads. Book early to secure your stay.

Currency & Payments

Moroccan Dirham (MAD) is the currency. ATMs are common; cards accepted widely, but keep cash handy for small expenses.
